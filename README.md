## ğŸ® Usage

1.  **Open Menu:** Press **`M`** (default keybinding) to open the Resource Tracker menu.
2.  **Create List:** Click **"Add New List"**.
3.  **Add Items:** Click the **Edit** button (middle of the list row) to open the editor.
    * Search for items on the left.
    * Click to add them to your tracker.
    * Set target amounts (optional).
4.  **Customize:** Use the input fields to change colors (ARGB) or scale.
5.  **Move HUD:** In the main menu, click **"Move HUD Elements"** to drag your lists to the perfect spot on your screen.
